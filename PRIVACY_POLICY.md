# Privacy Policy for Dinero

**Last Updated: February 25, 2026**

Dinero ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Local Data Storage:** Dinero is designed as a privacy-first application. By default, all financial data you enter (transactions, budgets, subscriptions) is stored **locally on your device**. We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly choose to backup or sync it with a cloud service.

**Analytics:** We use anonymized analytics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This may include information about app crashes, feature usage, and device type. This data does not contain personally identifiable financial information.

## 2. Permissions

To provide its features, Dinero may request the following permissions:
- **Notifications:** To send you reminders for bills, budgets, and spending insights.
- **Biometrics:** To secure your financial data using FaceID, TouchID, or Fingerprint.
- **Storage:** To export your data to CSV or PDF.

## 3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Since most data is stored on your device, the security of your data also depends on your device's security settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Dinero may use third-party services (such as Firebase for crash reporting) that collect information used to identify you. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Dinero does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
