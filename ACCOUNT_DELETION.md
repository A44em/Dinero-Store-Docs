# Account and Data Deletion

The **Dinero - Money Manager** application operates entirely **local-first** and prioritizes user privacy. 

Because we do not use, collect, or transmit any user accounts or application data to our own centralized servers, there is no remote "account" for us to delete. Your financial data lives entirely on your device.

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1: Clear Data Before Uninstalling (Recommended)
Because all your budgeting data and personalization settings are processed inside your device, you can completely erase them at any time:
1. Open your device's unified **Settings** application.
2. Go to **Apps** (Android) or **Storage** (iOS).
3. Find and tap on the **Dinero** app.
4. Select **Clear Data** / **Clear Storage**. This instantly wipes all transactions, accounts, and application settings.

### Option 2: Request Account Deletion via Email
If you would like to request the permanent deletion of your Firebase Authentication account associated with this application, you can do so by contacting us:
1. Send an email to **asimali1996@gmail.com**.
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request - Dinero"**.
3. Include the email address associated with your Dinero account in the body of the email.
4. We will process your request and permanently delete your authentication record within 7 business days.

### Option 3: Uninstall the App
Simply uninstalling or deleting the **Dinero** app from your smartphone will permanently securely erase your associated local application data and settings from the operating system.

## Data Retention Policy
- **What is deleted:** All transactions, personalized settings, categories, subscriptions, lock screen codes, and stored budgets.
- **What is kept by us:** None. As there are no central servers hosting user accounts, we retain exactly 0% of your personal or financial data.

If you have any further questions about our data practices, please review our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).
