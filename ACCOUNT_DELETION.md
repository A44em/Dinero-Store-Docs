# Account and Data Deletion

The **Dinero - Money Manager** application operates entirely **local-first** and prioritizes user privacy. 

Your financial data lives entirely on your device. We do not use, collect, or transmit any application data to our own centralized servers, meaning there is no remote "account data" for us to delete. 

However, if you choose to use the cloud backup or synchronization features, you may have created a Firebase Authentication account (via Email, Google, or Apple).

---

## 📱 How to Delete Your Local Financial Data

Because all your budgeting data and personalization settings are processed locally inside your device, you determine when they are erased:

### Option 1: Clear App Data (Recommended)
1. Open your device's unified **Settings** application.
2. Go to **Apps** (Android) or **Storage** (iOS).
3. Find and tap on the **Dinero** app.
4. Select **Clear Data** / **Clear Storage**. This instantly wipes all your local transactions, budgets, accounts, and application settings.

### Option 2: Uninstall the App
Simply uninstalling or deleting the **Dinero** app from your smartphone will permanently securely erase your associated local application data and settings from the operating system.

## ☁️ How to Delete Your Authentication Account

If you created an account (Email/Password, Google, or Apple) for app authentication, you can request its complete permanent removal from our authentication provider:

1. Send an email to **asimali1996@gmail.com**.
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request - Dinero"**.
3. Include the exact email address associated with your Dinero authentication account in the body of the email.
4. We will process your request and permanently delete your authentication record within 7 business days.

## Data Retention Policy
- **What is deleted locally:** All transactions, personalized settings, categories, subscriptions, lock screen codes, and stored budgets.
- **What is deleted in the cloud:** Your authentication profile (email address and sign-in credentials). 
- **What is kept by us:** None. As there are no central servers hosting user data, we retain exactly 0% of your personal or financial data.

If you have any further questions about our data practices, please review our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).
