# Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data from the **Dinero - Money Manager** application, please follow the instructions below. 

## How to Request Account Deletion

### Option 1: Deletion Within the App (Recommended)
You can directly delete your account and all associated data within the Dinero app:
1. Open the Dinero app on your device.
2. Navigate to the **Settings** tab.
3. Scroll down and select **Account Settings** (or find the **Delete Account** option).
4. Tap **Delete Account**.
5. Confirm your decision when prompted. This will immediately remove your account and all associated local and cloud data.

### Option 2: Request Deletion via Email
If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by contacting support:
1. Send an email to **asimali1996@gmail.com**.
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request - Dinero"**.
3. Include the email address associated with your Dinero account in the body of the email.
4. We will process your request and delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days.

## Data Retention Policy
When you delete your account (either via the app or by request), the following actions take place:
- **What is deleted:** Your user profile, authentication credentials (email/password), personalized settings, and all synced financial data (transactions, budgets, assets) stored on our servers will be permanently deleted.
- **What is kept:** We do not retain any of your personal or financial data after a successful deletion request. 
- **Local Data:** Please note that if you were using the app exclusively in offline mode, simply uninstalling the app from your device will permanently delete your local data.

If you have any further questions about our data practices, please review our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).
